About Sortes Vergilianae

The Sortes Vergilianae was a form of bibliomancy practiced in ancient Rome, where seekers would divine guidance by randomly selecting passages from Virgil's works, particularly the Aeneid.

The faithful believed that the chosen verse would illuminate their path, offering prophecy or counsel regarding their deepest concerns.
