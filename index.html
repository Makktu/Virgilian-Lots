About Sortes Vergilianae

The Sortes Vergilianae was a form of bibliomancy used in ancient Rome where individuals would seek guidance by randomly selecting passages from Virgil's works, particularly the Aeneid.

Followers believed that the randomly chosen verse would provide insight or prophecy regarding their question or concern.
